Local Cuisine

Stuffat tal-Fenek

Our national dish, rabbit stew. Slow cooked in rich tomatoe and wine sauce

Bragioli

Thin slices of beef wrapped around a stuffing of breadcrumbs, bacon, and herb

Ftira

A traditional Maltese flatbread, often topped with tomatoes, olives, tuna, and other Mediterranean ingredients

Pastizzi

Iconic Maltese savory pastries filled with ricotta cheese or mushy peas

Cisk

Our beloved local beer
